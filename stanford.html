Stanford

Coming to Stanford in 2023 for the MBA program has been the opportunity of a lifetime. There were two moments in my childhood when I knew I wanted to come here: first, when I read about Stanford's "What Matters Most to You and Why?" essay (which I spent over ten years thinking about before I applied), and second, when I read Phil Knight's (Stanford GSB Alum) Shoe Dog.

I'm enormously grateful for the chance to be a student at Stanford. It has broadened my horizons, introduced me to professors who are absolute legends as both thinkers and human beings, and surrounded me with friends and peers who challenge me, push me, and make me laugh :)
