Real Estate Investing

by Doug Abbey and Chris Mahowald

Understanding of the fundamentals of commercial real estate investment

  • One of the Most Fun Classes at the GSB: Going in, I had zero real estate experience and didn't quite know what to expect. But it quickly became a highlight.
  • Focused on Commercial Real Estate: We honed in on everything from industrial warehouses to office towers - dissecting what actually drives value in each segment.
  • Core concepts: Land economics, market/locality analysis, investment analysis, investment vehicles, real estate risk, and pitching to a RE IC. Major land uses discussed - apartment complexs, retail, office, and industrial.
  • A Balance of Art and Science: While property deals often hinge on relationships, gut feel, and market forces, there are also clear frameworks and metrics that help you evaluate an opportunity more objectively. I left feeling a lot more prepared to spot good deals‚Äîand negotiate an office lease, should the need arise.
